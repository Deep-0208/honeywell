### Photoshop Enhancement Tasks
**Source:** `public/images/home/hero/custom-hydraulic-cylinder-manufacturer.webp`

1. Open the 1024x1024 WebP image in Photoshop.
2. Change the Canvas Size to **1200x630px** (Anchor in the center).
3. Select the empty transparent areas on the left and right sides.
4. Use Generative Fill to seamlessly extend the background.
5. Export as JPEG, quality 85, max 300KB.
6. Save to `public/images/og/custom-hydraulic-cylinder-manufacturer-og.jpg`.

**Generative Fill Prompts (Photoshop AI):**
- "Clean white studio background" 
- "Extend industrial factory setting"
