## Content Strategy Framework

### The Revenue-First Content Model

```
TIER 1 — MONEY PAGES (Build First)
├── Homepage, Request Quote, Contact
├── Product Pillars (4 pages)
├── Key Location Pages (3 pages)
└── Goal: Generate inquiries from day 1

TIER 2 — PRODUCT PAGES (Build Second)
├── Individual product pages (19 pages)
└── Goal: Capture product-specific buyer searches

TIER 3 — SEGMENT PAGES (Build Third)
├── Application pages (7 pages)
├── Industry pages (11 pages)
└── Goal: Capture industry/application-specific searches

TIER 4 — LOCATION PAGES (Build Fourth)
├── Gujarat city pages (7 pages)
├── State/national pages (2 pages)
└── Goal: Dominate geo-targeted searches

TIER 5 — CONTENT PAGES (Ongoing)
├── Blog articles (25 initial)
├── Case studies (5 initial)
└── Goal: Build topical authority and capture informational traffic

TIER 6 — SUPPORT PAGES (Build Alongside)
├── About, Clients, Certifications, Gallery, FAQ, Downloads, Tools
└── Goal: Build E-E-A-T and support conversion from other pages
```

### Content Rules

| Rule | Specification |
|---|---|
| **One primary keyword per page** | No two pages compete for the same primary keyword |
| **Minimum word count** | Product: 800+ · Industry: 800+ · Blog: 1,500+ · Location: 600+ |
| **Every page has a CTA** | No dead-end pages. Every page drives toward a conversion. |
| **Every page links UP** | Every child page links to its parent pillar/hub |
| **Blog links to commerce** | Every blog article links to ≥ 2 product/application pages |
| **Real content only** | No lorem ipsum. No placeholder text. No AI-generated filler. |
