# 4. Material Certificates & Traceability

### Raw Material Certification
All primary structural materials used in pressure-bearing components are procured with material test certificates (MTCs) conforming to EN 10204 Type 3.1:

*   **Carbon Steel (ST52 / IS 2062):** Yield strength, tensile strength, elongation, and chemical composition certified per heat batch
*   **Alloy Steel (EN24T / 42CrMo4):** Heat-treatment certification confirming achieved hardness and strength values
*   **Aluminium Alloy (6061-T6):** Chemical composition and mechanical property certification per batch

### Component-Level Traceability
Each pressure-bearing component is identifiable to its raw material heat batch through:
*   Laser-engraved heat codes on cylinder barrels, rods, and manifold blocks
*   Internal job card system linking each finished component to its material certificate, machining records, and test results
*   Digital records maintained for a minimum of [PLACEHOLDER_YEARS] years from despatch date

# 5. Pressure Testing Certification

### Hydrostatic Test Certificates
Every hydraulic cylinder and manifold block manufactured at Honeywell Hydraulics is subject to a witnessed hydrostatic pressure test at 1.5× maximum rated working pressure. The test certificate issued with each component documents:

*   Component description and serial/batch number
*   Rated working pressure (Bar)
*   Test pressure applied (Bar = 1.5× rated)
*   Test duration (minimum [PLACEHOLDER_MINUTES] minutes at test pressure)
*   Result: Pass / Fail
*   Tester's name and signature
*   Date of test

### Third-Party Witnessed Testing
For projects requiring independent verification (infrastructure, defence, export), we can arrange third-party witnessed pressure testing by:
*   Bureau Veritas (BV)
*   SGS India
*   TÜV SÜD South Asia
*   DNV India

Third-party test certification is available at additional cost and requires advance notice for scheduling.

# 7. Seal & Fluid Compatibility Approvals

Our standard seal packages are validated for use with the following hydraulic fluid types:
*   **Mineral Oil (ISO VG 46, VG 68):** All standard Nitrile (NBR) and Polyurethane seal packages
*   **High Water Base Fluids (HWBF):** Available on request with EPDM sealing
*   **Phosphate Ester (HFD):** Available with Viton (FKM) sealing
*   **Biodegradable (HETG / HEPG):** Available with specialist seal specification

*Custom seal specifications for non-standard fluids are available. Contact engineering@honeywellhydraulics.com with your fluid specification.*
