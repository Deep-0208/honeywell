# 3. The Honeywell Fluid Power Ecosystem

Industrial automation requires more than just individual components; it requires a synchronized fluid power ecosystem. At Honeywell Hydraulics, we manufacture every critical node of that ecosystem under one roof, ensuring absolute compatibility, minimal pressure drops, and maximum mechanical efficiency.

**How Our Products Work Together:**
In a typical high-tonnage industrial application, our **Hydraulic Power Packs** act as the heart of the system, converting electrical energy into pressurized hydrostatic energy. This pressurized fluid is routed through our custom CNC-machined **Manifold Blocks**, which act as the brain, housing the control valves that dictate the direction, pressure, and flow of the fluid. Finally, the fluid reaches our heavy-duty **Hydraulic Cylinders**—the muscle of the operation—converting the hydraulic pressure into precise, unstoppable linear force. 

For clients who require end-to-end automation, our **Hydraulic Systems** division engineers the entire loop, integrating our proprietary cylinders, manifolds, and power packs with advanced PLC electrical controls for a complete turnkey solution.

# 4. Product Categories Grid

We manufacture four core families of industrial hydraulic equipment. Select a category below to explore technical specifications, mounting configurations, and variant options.

## A. [Hydraulic Cylinders](/hydraulic-cylinders/)
Hydraulic cylinders are mechanical actuators used to provide unidirectional force through a unidirectional stroke. Our cylinders are CNC-machined from ST52 seamless tubes, featuring micro-honed barrels and hard-chrome plated EN8 rods.
*   **Summary:** Heavy-duty linear actuators available in custom bore sizes from 40mm to 300mm.
*   **Variants:** Double Acting, Single Acting, Flange Mounting, Clevis Mounting, Tie-Rod, Telescopic.
*   **Use Cases:** Press machine actuation, injection moulding core pulls, automated material handling, and heavy lifting.
*   **[EXPLORE HYDRAULIC CYLINDERS →](/hydraulic-cylinders/)**

## B. [Hydraulic Power Packs](/hydraulic-power-packs/)
The standalone power units that generate fluid flow. Built with premium gear or vane pumps, high-efficiency industrial motors, and robust steel reservoirs, our power packs provide consistent, aerated-free hydraulic pressure.
*   **Summary:** Pre-assembled, piped, and tested fluid power generation units.
*   **Variants:** 3-Phase Industrial Units, Single-Phase Units, Dedicated Press Machine High-Low Circuits, Goods Lift Units.
*   **Use Cases:** Continuous factory operations, hydraulic press machinery, commercial passenger lifts, and automated parking systems.
*   **[EXPLORE HYDRAULIC POWER PACKS →](/hydraulic-power-packs/)**

## C. [Manifold Blocks](/manifold-blocks/)
Machined from solid blocks of aluminum or steel, our manifold blocks consolidate your hydraulic circuitry. By integrating multiple control valves into a single block, we eliminate messy external piping and drastically reduce potential leak points.
*   **Summary:** Precision CNC-machined hydraulic valve housings for circuit consolidation.
*   **Variants:** Standard 06 Size, Standard 10 Size, Multi-Station Blocks, Single-Station Blocks.
*   **Use Cases:** Complex hydraulic circuits requiring multiple directional, flow, and pressure control valves in a confined footprint.
*   **[EXPLORE MANIFOLD BLOCKS →](/manifold-blocks/)**

# 5. Product Selection Guide

Choosing the correct industrial hydraulic equipment requires careful calculation of your operational parameters. Use this guide to navigate our product hub:

**1. Do you need to generate physical motion or force?**
*   If you need linear push/pull force, you need a **[Hydraulic Cylinder](/hydraulic-cylinders/)**.
*   *Key consideration:* Determine your required tonnage (Force = Pressure × Piston Area) to select the correct bore size.

**2. Do you need to generate pressurized fluid flow?**
*   If you are building a new machine or replacing a failing pump system, you need a **[Hydraulic Power Pack](/hydraulic-power-packs/)**.
*   *Key consideration:* Determine your required cycle speed (Flow Rate/LPM) and system pressure (Bar) to size the motor and pump correctly.

**3. Do you need to control complex fluid routing?**
*   If your machine has multiple cylinders acting in sequence, or if you are trying to eliminate leaky steel piping, you need a **[Manifold Block](/manifold-blocks/)**.
*   *Key consideration:* Determine the number of valve stations (06 or 10 size) required for your directional control valves.

**4. Do you need a complete machine upgrade?**
*   If you lack in-house fluid power engineers, utilize our **[Hydraulic Systems](/hydraulic-systems/)** division. We will take your functional requirements and deliver a complete, plug-and-play automated system.
