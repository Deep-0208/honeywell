# 3. Material Handling Hydraulic Challenges

*   **Safety-Critical Load Holding:** A goods lift or scissor table that drifts downward under load is not merely a performance failure — it is a life-safety failure. Every lifting application requires dedicated pilot-operated check valves or counterbalance valves directly at the cylinder to provide mechanical load-holding independent of the hydraulic circuit's pressure integrity.
*   **Smooth, Judder-Free Motion:** Operators and goods on a lifting platform experience every jerk and lurch in the hydraulic actuation. Smooth lifting requires proportional flow control and correctly specified regeneration circuits to prevent flow stepping and velocity jumps.
*   **High Duty Cycles:** A goods lift in an e-commerce fulfilment warehouse may cycle 500+ times per day. This demands seals and cylinders specified for high-cycle industrial service, not standard commercial lift grades.
*   **Compact Installation:** Material handling equipment is installed in finished buildings with strict height and footprint constraints. Compact [DC Mini Power Packs](/hydraulic-power-packs/dc-mini-power-packs/) and telescopic cylinders minimize the installed footprint.

# 4. Material Handling Applications

### Hydraulic Goods Lifts
Single and double-cylinder hydraulic lift platforms for goods transfer between floors. We design the complete hydraulic system — power unit, manifold with integrated load-holding and smooth lowering valves, and the cylinder(s) — and size all components for the specified rated load and lift height.

### Scissor Lift Tables
Factory floor and loading dock scissor lift tables using one or two [Welded Hydraulic Cylinders](/hydraulic-cylinders/welded-hydraulic-cylinders/). We specify cylinders with anti-creep counterbalance valves mounted directly at the cylinder port and power packs with thermal cut-out protection for high-frequency duty.

### Dock Levellers
Hydraulic dock leveller platforms that bridge the gap between loading dock and truck bed. These applications require a compact, reliable power unit and a simple, robust cylinder that can survive the physical abuse of forklift traffic.

### Telescopic Boom Systems
Material handling cranes and reach stackers using [Telescopic Hydraulic Cylinders](/hydraulic-cylinders/telescopic-hydraulic-cylinders/) for multi-stage extension. We supply telescopic cylinders with integrated staging logic for controlled, sequence-guaranteed extension.

### AGV & Conveyor Systems
Hydraulic elevation and routing mechanisms for Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) systems and powered conveyor junctions requiring precise, fast-acting hydraulic position control.

# 9. FAQ

**Q: Is a pilot-operated check valve sufficient for goods lift load-holding, or do we need a counterbalance valve?**
**A:** Both provide load-holding, but a counterbalance valve is the superior choice for lifting applications. A POCV holds load only while the pilot signal is maintained. A counterbalance valve provides positive load-holding regardless of the pilot line condition and also provides over-running load protection (preventing runaway descent if the load drives the cylinder faster than the pump can supply oil). We specify counterbalance valves on all goods lift and personnel lift applications as a safety standard.

**Q: Our scissor lift is jerking at the start and end of each stroke. How do we fix this?**
**A:** Jerking (stick-slip) is caused by static friction in the cylinder seals breaking free suddenly, causing a rapid fluid demand that the system cannot immediately meet. Solutions include specifying low-friction PTFE-based seal packages, adding a flow ramp valve to control acceleration, or installing an accumulator to provide instantaneous flow buffering at the start of each stroke.
