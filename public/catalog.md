- **Mounting Styles:** Flange, Clevis, Trunnion, Tie-Rod (8 standard types)

## 2. Hydraulic Power Packs
Robust fluid power units for continuous plant operations and heavy machinery.

- **Types:** 3-Phase and Single-Phase units
- **Customization:** Flow rates and pressures tailored to exact machinery requirements
- **Features:** Judder-free operation, optimal thermal management, High-Low pump configurations
- **Applications:** Injection Moulding, Presses, Material Handling

## 3. Hydraulic Manifold Blocks
Custom-machined blocks for integrated and space-efficient hydraulic circuits.

- **Features:** Precision-drilled ports, optimized internal flow paths
- **Benefits:** Minimizes external piping, reduces leak points, compact design
- **Compatibility:** Configurable for specific directional, pressure, and flow control valves

## 4. Custom Hydraulic Systems
Complete turnkey hydraulic solutions — from engineering to commissioning.

- **Process:** Load & duty cycle analysis -> CAD Engineering -> CNC Machining -> Testing
- **Applications:** Factory Automation, Rolling Mills, Construction Equipment, Earthmoving
