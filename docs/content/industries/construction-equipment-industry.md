# 3. Construction Equipment Hydraulic Challenges

*   **Extreme Mechanical Abuse:** Construction sites subject hydraulic cylinders to constant side-loading, impact from falling debris, and operation in dust, mud, and water. Cylinders must be engineered for physical durability, not just pressure capacity.
*   **Weather Exposure:** Equipment operating outdoors in monsoon conditions requires IP67+ rated electrical components, UV-resistant hoses, and hard-chrome cylinder rods with corrosion-resistant seal packages.
*   **Engine-Dependent Hydraulics:** Unlike factory equipment with a stable electric motor driving the pump, construction equipment uses engine-driven PTOs where pump flow varies with engine RPM. Systems must be designed to function correctly across the engine's full RPM range.
*   **Field Serviceability:** Construction equipment breaks down in remote locations far from service workshops. Components must be simple to diagnose, easy to remove, and replaceable with standardized parts available from local suppliers.

# 4. Construction Equipment Applications

### Tipper Truck Body Hoist Systems
The most volume-significant construction hydraulic application. [Telescopic Hydraulic Cylinders](/hydraulic-cylinders/telescopic-hydraulic-cylinders/) (3–5 stage) lift the dump body of tipper trucks. We manufacture telescopic cylinders for 10-tonne to 60-tonne tipper capacities, with heavy-duty weld-on base eyes, oversize stage diameters for rated side-load capacity, and Nitrile or Viton seal packages for all-weather operation.

### Excavator & Backhoe Boom/Stick/Bucket Cylinders
Heavy-duty double-acting [Welded Hydraulic Cylinders](/hydraulic-cylinders/welded-hydraulic-cylinders/) for excavator boom, stick, and bucket functions. We supply replacement cylinders to OEM dimensions for the Indian excavator market, with re-chrome rod capability for field-damaged cylinders.

### Crane & Telescopic Handler Systems
[Mobile Hydraulic Systems](/hydraulic-systems/mobile-hydraulic-systems/) for truck-mounted cranes (PICs) and rough-terrain telehandlers, including load-sensing variable-displacement circuits, anti-cavitation valves, and overload protection.

### Concrete Transit Mixer Drum Drive
Hydraulic motor drive systems for concrete transit mixer drums. The drum must maintain constant rotation direction and speed regardless of engine RPM variation during transit. We design closed-loop speed-controlled hydraulic motor circuits for this application.

# 10. FAQ

**Q: Can you manufacture telescopic cylinders to replace imported cylinders on our existing tippers?**
**A:** Yes. Provide us with the existing cylinder's retracted length, extended length, bore sizes of each stage, port thread specification, and mounting eye dimensions. We will manufacture a dimensionally identical replacement in India.

**Q: What is the correct seal material for a tipper cylinder operating in monsoon conditions?**
**A:** For outdoor construction equipment, we recommend Viton (FKM) wiper seals and buffer seals as the minimum specification for the rod sealing system. Viton provides superior resistance to water ingress, UV radiation, and ozone compared to standard Nitrile — significantly extending seal service life in outdoor duty.
