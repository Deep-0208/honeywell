# 3. Company Overview

Established in Ahmedabad, Honeywell Hydraulics has grown into a highly trusted **hydraulic engineering company**. We do not simply assemble off-the-shelf components; we are a primary fluid power manufacturer. We transform raw ST52 steel tubes and EN8 rods into high-precision hydraulic cylinders, custom power packs, and synchronized manifold systems that automate the heavy lifting and high-tonnage pressing of modern industry.

Our headquarters and manufacturing facility are strategically located in Kathwada GIDC, allowing us to serve as the immediate, reliable supply chain partner for OEMs and machine builders across [Gujarat](/locations/gujarat/) and PAN-India.

---

# 4. Manufacturing Philosophy

We believe that in the industrial B2B sector, equipment failure is unacceptable. A leaking cylinder or a stalled power pack doesn't just cost the price of the part; it halts an entire production line, costing our clients thousands in lost revenue.

Therefore, our manufacturing philosophy is built entirely around **Zero Defect Engineering**. We achieve this by never outsourcing our core competencies. By maintaining absolute in-house control over CAD design, CNC precision turning, micro-honing, seal assembly, and final pressure testing, we remove the variables that lead to mechanical failure.

---

# 5. Engineering Excellence

The difference between a generic hydraulic supplier and an engineering partner lies in the mathematical precision applied before the metal is ever cut. 

Before manufacturing begins, our application engineers perform comprehensive kinematic profiling. We calculate the exact bore size needed to achieve your pressing tonnage, determine the ideal pump flow rate to match your required cycle speed, and simulate the exact fluid dynamics to prevent system overheating. This commitment to engineering excellence ensures that every product we deliver is perfectly optimized for its final application.

# 8. Quality & Testing Overview

Quality is not an abstract concept; it is quantifiable data. We operate under stringent quality management protocols that dictate dimensional inspections at every stage of the manufacturing lifecycle. 

Crucially, we do not rely on random batch testing. **100% of the hydraulic cylinders and power packs we produce are hydrostatically tested at 1.5x their maximum working pressure before dispatch.** If a cylinder is rated for 210 Bar, we test it at 315 Bar to guarantee structural integrity and zero internal bypass.
