# 5. How We Customize Solutions By Industry

When we engineer custom hydraulic solutions, we adapt three critical variables based on your industry profile:

1.  **Metallurgy & Plating:** A cylinder used in chemical processing requires specialized anti-corrosive coatings, whereas a cylinder in a steel plant requires high-tensile ST52 steel to survive shock loads. We adapt the raw material to the environment.
2.  **Sealing Technology:** Standard nitrile seals fail rapidly in high-temperature or highly abrasive environments. We customize every unit with industry-specific seals (PTFE, Viton, or heavy-duty Polyurethane) imported from leading global manufacturers.
3.  **Circuit Logic:** A hydraulic press requires rapid approach speeds followed by slow, massive force. We customize our power packs and manifold blocks with specific high-low valving logic to perfectly match your machine's cycle profile.
