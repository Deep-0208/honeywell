**Client:** [PLACEHOLDER CLIENT], Leading Textile Processor
**Location:** Pandesara GIDC, Surat

**The Challenge:**
A major fabric processing plant in Pandesara was experiencing chronic overheating in their imported hydraulic systems. Running 24/7, the hydraulic fluid temperature frequently exceeded 70°C, causing catastrophic pump failures every four months.

**The Solution:**
Honeywell engineers performed an on-site audit in Surat. We completely retrofitted the machine with a custom **3-Phase Hydraulic Power Pack** manufactured in our Gujarat facility. **The Outcome:**
Hydraulic fluid temperatures stabilized at a constant 42°C. The plant has operated for 18 months without a single pump failure, eliminating the massive downtime costs previously associated with the machine.
