# Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to the most common questions our engineering and sales teams receive regarding our products, manufacturing processes, and shipping.

## Ordering & Lead Times

**What is your standard production lead time?**
Standard catalog hydraulic cylinders and basic power packs typically ship within 2-4 weeks. Custom engineered systems and OEM First Article prototypes usually require [PLACEHOLDER_LEAD_TIME] weeks from drawing approval.

**Do you accept custom hydraulic cylinder designs?**
Yes. We specialize in [custom hydraulic system design](/services/custom-hydraulic-system-design/). We can manufacture to your exact CAD drawings or engineer a completely new cylinder based on your operational parameters.

**Is there a minimum order quantity (MOQ)?**
For standard sizes and custom one-off repairs, there is no MOQ. For dedicated [OEM manufacturing agreements](/services/oem-hydraulic-manufacturing/), we typically look for batch sizes that justify dedicated CNC setup times.

## Warranty & Quality

**What is your warranty policy?**
We offer a standard [PLACEHOLDER_WARRANTY_MONTHS]-month manufacturing defect warranty on all new hydraulic cylinders and power packs. [Cylinder repairs](/services/hydraulic-cylinder-repair/) carry a dedicated workmanship warranty.

**How do you test your cylinders before shipping?**
Every single cylinder undergoes 100% hydrostatic testing at 1.5x its rated working pressure on our dedicated test rigs before it is painted and dispatched.

## Service & Support

**Do you provide field repair services?**
We primarily perform repairs at our Kathwada GIDC facility in Ahmedabad, where we have the necessary honing machines, lathes, and testing equipment to ensure a perfect rebuild. 

**Do you ship outside of Gujarat?**
Yes, we supply OEMs and industrial clients [across India](/locations/india/). We ensure heavy-duty wooden crating to prevent transit damage.
